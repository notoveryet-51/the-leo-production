Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about the editing process? Here are some quick answers.

Reels and short-form content are usually delivered in 3-5 business days. Long-form video editing (such as wedding highlights or music videos) typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on the complexity.

We offer 2 rounds of revisions on all packages to adjust cuts, color grading, sound design, or text overlays. Clear feedback helps ensure we match your creative vision perfectly.

You can upload your raw video assets to Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer and share the link with us via email or Instagram message. Organizing files into folders is highly appreciated!

We edit primarily on Adobe Premiere Pro and DaVinci Resolve for premium color grading, along with Adobe After Effects for complex visual transitions and motion graphics.
